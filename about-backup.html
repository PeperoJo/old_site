about me
a little bit about myself...

Hi, I'm Joseph Han. I am currently a sophomore at the University of Notre Dame. While I plan on majoring in computer science, I am also interested in graphic design and studio art. Before coming to Notre Dame, I went to Stuyvesant High School, a specialized high school located in downtown Manhattan (New York City). During my time there, I gained interest in science, mathematics, and computer science. I also got inspired from logo designing and taught myself how to use Photoshop, Illustrator, and other programs.
